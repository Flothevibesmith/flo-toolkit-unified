# Flo's Toolkit — Cheat Sheet

Say it naturally, English or Romanian — these are just the short forms. It works on
every message; these five are the big jobs.

| You want | Say | RO |
|---|---|---|
| Understand something | **explain this to me** | explică-mi |
| Notes from a PDF/slides | **read this for me** | fă-mi notițe |
| 5-minute quiz | **quiz me** | testează-mă |
| Full exam prep | **make my exam kit** | pregătește-mă de examen |
| Essay feedback | **review my draft** | vezi eseul meu |

**Saving:** there is none. It remembers automatically (that's the memory setting
from setup Step 1). Backup anytime: **export my progress**.

**When it asks setup questions before a big job:** answer with taps like **1b 2a**,
or just say **defaults**. Say **C** if you just want your original question answered.

**Phone:** everything works, same account. · **Skip any quiz question:** **skip**.
**Stuck or weird?** → **is it working?**
